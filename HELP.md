## How to Use This Calculator

1. Select an investment strategy on the Calculator page.
2. Enter the required parameters (dates, tickers, amounts).
3. Click Calculate to fetch prices and compute the results.
4. Review the summary metrics, interactive chart, and detailed table.
5. Download the results as CSV if needed.

### Notes
- Dates cannot be in the future.
- For RSI Swing Strategy, provide a comma-separated list of tickers.
- Fractional shares are supported.

## Available Strategies

- Simple Recurring Strategy — Dollar-cost averaging with a single stock  
  [Read the strategy README](strategies/simple_recurring.md)

- RSI Swing Strategy — Selects stock with best RSI and switches on drawdown  
  [Read the strategy README](strategies/rsi_swing.md)

## Outputs
- Principal Invested
- Total Account Value
- Profit/Loss
- Optional: Current Stock (for multi-stock strategies)

## Troubleshooting
- If no data is found for the requested range, check the dates and tickers.
- Ensure your internet connection is available for fetching market data.
